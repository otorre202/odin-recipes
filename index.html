Pizza

Description

This pizza recipe is simple yet flavorful, combining a crispy crust with a delicious blend of cheese and toppings. It's a favorite among kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Your favorite toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven.
  2. Roll out dough.
  3. Apply tomato sauce.
  4. Add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake until crispy and golden brown.

Ice Cream

Description

This homemade ice cream recipe is rich, creamy, and can be made with just a few ingredients. It's a perfect treat for a hot summer day or a special occasion.

Ingredients

  • Cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Mix cream and sugar until sugar dissolves.
  2. Add vanilla extract.
  3. Freeze until solid.
